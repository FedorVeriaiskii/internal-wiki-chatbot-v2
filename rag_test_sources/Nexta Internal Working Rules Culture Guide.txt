# Nexta Internal Working Rules & Culture Guide

Welcome to the **Nexta** team! We believe that clear expectations lead to better work and fewer "wait, was I supposed to do that?" moments. These rules aren't meant to be a straightjacket—they are the framework that allows our creativity to flourish.

---

## 1. The Daily Rhythm (Hours & Presence)
At Nexta, we focus on **outcomes over hours**. However, to ensure we can actually collaborate without playing 24-hour tag, we follow these guidelines:

* **Core Collaboration Hours:** While we offer flexible start times, all team members are expected to be online and available between **10:00 AM and 3:00 PM (Local Time)**.
* **Standard Workday:** A typical day is 8 hours. Most "Nextans" settle into a **9:00 AM – 5:00 PM** or **8:30 AM – 4:30 PM** groove.
* **Status Updates:** If you’re stepping away for more than 30 minutes (gym, school run, or a very long sandwich), please update your Slack status so your teammates aren't shouting into the void.

## 2. Rest & Recharge (Vacations & Leave)
A burnt-out Nextan is a sad Nextan. We want you to use your time off.

* **PTO Accrual:** Full-time employees accrue **20 days of Paid Time Off** per year, plus public holidays.
* **The "Nexta Recharge":** The office is closed the entire week between Christmas and New Year’s Day. This does *not* count against your PTO.
* **Booking Leave:** For any trip longer than two days, please submit your request in the HR portal at least **two weeks in advance**. We rarely say no, but we do need to make sure the ship keeps sailing.
* **Sick Days:** If you’re unwell, **stay offline**. Rest is the only way to get back to 100%. No "heroics" required.

## 3. Communication Etiquette
We move fast, but we don't want to drown in notifications.

* **Slack vs. Email:** Use Slack for quick syncs and "vibe checks." Use Email for formal approvals, client-facing communication, and anything that needs a permanent paper trail.
* **The 24-Hour Rule:** We aim to acknowledge internal messages within one business day. If it’s an emergency, use the "URGENT" tag (but use it sparingly).
* **Meeting-Free Wednesdays:** To encourage "Deep Work," we discourage internal meetings on Wednesdays. Put your head down and build something cool.

## 4. Workspace & Dress Code
* **The "Nexta" Look:** Our dress code is **"Dress for your Day."** If you’re meeting a high-level client, maybe skip the flip-flops. If you’re coding in the corner all day, hoodies are basically our unofficial uniform.
* **Hybrid Setup:** For those in hybrid roles, we congregate in the office on **Tuesdays and Thursdays** for catered lunches and face-to-face brainstorming.

---

> **The Golden Rule:** When in doubt, communicate. We’re all adults here—if you need to shift your schedule or take a mental health afternoon, just let your lead know. We’ve got your back.
